FAQ

What types of office space does AJS Ventures offer?
We offer a range of office solutions tailored to meet diverse needs of our clients and accommodate businesses of all shapes and sizes. If you need your own dedicated space and complete personalization and privacy, then you can choose between a private office with private amenities (kitchen, entertainment zones, dining space, lounge, collaboration space). However, if you have a smaller team or more teams but you only need a dedicated work space, you can choose a private office with shared amenities or rent an office space to fit what you need that you can scale as your business grows. Shared amenities include facilities like meeting rooms and kitchen spaces, lounge and dining area. This arrangement blends the advantages of a private office with the cost-effectiveness and collaborative benefits of shared amenities.
